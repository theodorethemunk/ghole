Mileena Glory Hole or High Risk High Reward Hole Animation is a lewd rule 34 POV animation by howlSFM depicting a person sticking their finger into Mortal Kombat character Mileena's mouth from behind a glory hole, followed by Mileena sucking their finger.

The animation was published online in March 2022 and became the subject of memes on TikTok in June 2023, becoming known as the "HIGH RISK HIGH REWARD" animation.
